COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
✗ Wrong dimensions - Must be EXACT pixel sizes
✗ Rounded corners on app icon - Play Store adds them automatically
✗ Text too close to edges - Leave 5% safe margin
✗ CMYK color space - Must convert to RGB
✗ Compressed screenshots - Use PNG-24, not heavily compressed
✗ Wrong aspect ratio - Screenshots must be 9:16 (1080×1920)
✗ Outdated screenshots - Must show current app version
✗ Mixed image types - Be consistent with PNG or JPEG
