## 6. Permissions & Device Access

### 6.1 Required Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

**Internet Access (`INTERNET`)**
- **Purpose:** To connect to Firebase servers for authentication and data synchronization
- **When Collected:** Whenever the App connects to our servers
- **Impact:** Required for core App functionality

**Post Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`)**
- **Purpose:** To send reminder notifications for your tasks, expenses, events, and payments
- **When Collected:** When you schedule reminders
- **Impact:** Required for notification functionality (Android 13+)

**Receive Boot Completed (`RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`)**
- **Purpose:** To restore scheduled reminders when your device starts
- **When Collected:** Only when device is powered on
- **Impact:** Ensures reminders persist after device restart

**Schedule Exact Alarm (`SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`)**
- **Purpose:** To trigger reminders at precise scheduled times
- **When Collected:** When alarm manager schedules reminders
- **Impact:** Improves reminder timing accuracy (Android 12+)

### 6.2 Permission Management
You can manage permissions at any time through:
1. Android Settings → Apps → Pocket App → Permissions
2. Grant or deny specific permissions as needed
3. Disabling a permission may limit certain App features
